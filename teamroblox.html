Instructions on how to make a team for your game on Roblox

Find the 'View' tab

Near the middle, you'll find 'Team Create' click on it.

On the bottom left corner, you'll find a green button saying 'Turn On' click it.

If you wait for a moment or a minute, after the 'Requesting Access' your Team Access will be enabled.

On the bottom left corner again, you'll find 'Invite a friend' type the username of the person.

Wait for them to accept, and there's a chat for you to use to communicate to your partner.

You can invite at least 2 players to join (they must be added as your friends)
