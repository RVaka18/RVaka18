Word: Definition:
rational number a number that can be written as a ratio of two integers a and b, where b is not zero.
terminating decimals decimals that come to an end
repeating decimals one or more digits that repeat infintely.
set a group of items
subset contained of the second set
opposite or additive inverse is the same distance from 0 on a number line as the original number, but on the other side of 0.
















Word: Definition:
unit rates comparison of two quantities that have different units, such as miles and hours. Rates are often expressed as unit rates, that is, with a denominator of 1 unit.
complex fraction a fraction that has a fraction in its numerator, denominator, or both.
rate of change rate that describes how one quantity changes in relation to another quantity.
proportional relationship between two quantities is one in which the rate of change is constant, or one in which the ratio of one quantity to the other is constant.
proportion a statement that two rates or ratios are equivalent
constant or proportionality y = kx, it represents the constant rate of change
















Word: Definition:
conversion factor ratio of two equivalent measurements
Percent increase describes how much a quantity increase in comparison to the original amount
Percent decrease describes how much a quantity decreases in comparison to the original amount
Simple interest fixed percent of the principal.
principal original amount of money borrowed or deposited
















Word: Definition:
Similar Shapes have the same shape, but not necessarily the same size.
Corresponding angles and correspoding sides two or more similar shapes are in the same relative position
indirect measurement using similar shapes and proportions to find a measure
radius line segment with one endpoint at the center of the circle and the other endpoint of the circle.
diameter a line segment that passes through the center of the circle and whose endpoints lie on the circle. The length of the diameter is twice the length of the radius.
circumference circle is the distance around the circle.
















Word: Definition:
experiment an activity involving chance in which results are observed
trial Each observation of an experiment
outcome each result
event a set of one or more outcomes is an event.
Probability measures the likelihood that the event will occur, written P(event)
sample space set of all possible outcomes for an event.
complement is the set of all outcomes in the sample space that are not included in the event.
experimental probability an event that is found by comparing the number of times the event occurs to the total number of trials.
simple event when there is only one outcome for an event
simulation model of an experiment that would be difficult or inconvenient to actually perform
compound event an event that includes two or more simple events, such as flipping a coin and rolling a number cube.
Theoretical probability probability that an event occurs when all the outcomes of the experiment are equally likely.

Word: Definition:
net two-dimensional pattern of shapes that can be folded into a three-dimensional figure.
pyramid three-dimensional shape whose base is a polygon and whose other faces are all triangles.
lateral faces parallelograms that connect the bases. Each face that is not a base is a lateral face.
lateral area sum of the areas of all the lateral faces
surface area sum of areas of all the surfaces of a figure expressed in square units.
